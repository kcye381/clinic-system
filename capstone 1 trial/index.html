About MediCare Clinic

For over a decade, we've been committed to providing exceptional healthcare services with compassion, innovation, and excellence.

2010

Our Humble Beginnings

MediCare Clinic started as a small family practice with just 3 doctors and a vision to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone.

2013

Expansion & Growth

We expanded our facility and added specialized departments including Cardiology, Neurology, and Pediatrics.

2016

Digital Transformation

Launched our online appointment system, becoming one of the first clinics in the region to offer digital healthcare management.

2020

Pandemic Response

Implemented telemedicine services and safety protocols to continue serving our community during challenging times.

2024

State-of-the-Art Facility

Now serving over 50,000 patients annually with 25+ specialized doctors and cutting-edge medical technology.
